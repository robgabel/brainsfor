_Exploration doc following the Lunour / Lexicon playbook. Target: 300+ candidates, then narrow._

## The Brief

**What it is:** Packaged "brain packs" — curated knowledge from notable thinkers that users install into their AI. Each brain ships with 8 thinking skills (`/advise`, `/teach`, `/debate`, `/connect`, `/evolve`, `/surprise`, `/coach`, `/predict`).

**Benefit ladder**

| Rung | |
|---|---|
| Feature | Curated atoms + connections + skills packaged as a file |
| Functional | Your AI thinks like Belsky / Graham / Attia on demand |
| Emotional | Borrowed judgment. A mentor in your ear. Standing on shoulders of giants |
| **Ultimate** | **Inherited wisdom. A consultable mind.** |

**Three adjectives we want people to feel:** *confident, curious, elevated.*

**We're NOT going there**
- `*AI`, `*GPT`, `*Mind`, `*Brain`, `*Think` (saturation zone)
- Literal stems: knowledge, skill, pack, wisdom, smart, expert
- SaaS-neutral soup: Nexis, Sync, Flow, Hub, Stack
- Anything describing what the product does

**Sound targets**
- 1–3 syllables (2 is ideal)
- Hard front consonant (K, V, M, T, P) for confidence
- Open vowel finish for memorability
- Must survive being said over a bad Zoom call

## Recommended next steps

1. **Domain + trademark sweep** on the 15 finalists — Instant Domain Search + USPTO TESS
2. **One-on-one reactions** (not a focus group) — 10 people each
3. **Story test** — can you write a 2-sentence origin story for each? The ones that resist storytelling are weaker than they seem
4. **Say it 20 times** — Mentra vs. Sagent on a bad Zoom; which still lands?
5. **Cross-brand fit** — "Belsky's brain on *Mentra*" vs. "Belsky's brain on *Sagent*" — which makes the thinker-first model sing?

## Two honest gut calls

- **Mentra** has more poetry. Invented words age better (Swiffer, Vercel, Kodak). Feels like a brand, not a product category.
- **Sagent** is riding the agent wave — which is an asset *today* and a risk in 3–5 years if "agent" becomes the "cloud" of the 2020s (ubiquitous and invisible). It also tells people what it is a little too clearly, flirting with the invisible zone.

If forced to pick one for Day 1: **Mentra**. If optimizing for "Product Hunt launches in the next 12 months recognize this instantly": **Sagent**.
