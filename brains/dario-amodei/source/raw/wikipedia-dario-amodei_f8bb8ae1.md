**Dario Amodei** (born 1983) is an American [artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artificial_intelligence "Artificial intelligence") (AI) researcher and entrepreneur. In 2021, he and his sister [Daniela Amodei](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniela_Amodei "Daniela Amodei") co-founded [Anthropic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anthropic "Anthropic"), the company behind the [large language model](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Large_language_model "Large language model") series [Claude](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Claude_(language_model) "Claude (language model)").[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-2) Prior to that, he was the vice president of research at [OpenAI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OpenAI "OpenAI").[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-3)[\[4\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-4)

In his capacity as Anthropic's [CEO](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chief_executive_officer "Chief executive officer"), Amodei often writes on the benefits and risks of advanced AI systems.[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:4-5) He is a proponent of an "entente" strategy in which a coalition of democratic nations use advanced AI systems in military applications to achieve a decisive advantage over adversaries while sharing the benefits with cooperating nations.[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:0-6)[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:1-7)[\[8\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:2-8)

## Early life and education

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Dario_Amodei&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Early life and education")\]

Dario Amodei was born in [San Francisco](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/San_Francisco "San Francisco"), California, in 1983.[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:3-1) His sister, [Daniela](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Daniela_Amodei "Daniela Amodei"), was born four years later.[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:3-1) His father, Riccardo Amodei, an Italian-American leather craftsman from [Massa Marittima](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Massa_Marittima "Massa Marittima"), [Tuscany](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tuscany "Tuscany"), died when Amodei was a young adult.[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-9)[\[10\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-10) His mother, Elena Engel, a Jewish American born in [Chicago](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chicago "Chicago"), worked as a [project manager](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Project_manager "Project manager") for libraries.[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:3-1)

Dario grew up in San Francisco and graduated from [Lowell High School](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lowell_High_School_(San_Francisco) "Lowell High School (San Francisco)").[\[11\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-11) He was a member of the [USA Physics Olympiad team](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States_Physics_Olympiad "United States Physics Olympiad") in 2000.[\[12\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-Physics_Olympics-2000-12) Amodei began college at [Caltech](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Caltech "Caltech"), where he worked with [Tom Tombrello](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tom_Tombrello "Tom Tombrello") as one of his Physics 11 students. He later transferred to [Stanford University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stanford_University "Stanford University"), where he received a bachelor of science degree in [physics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Physics "Physics").[\[13\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-13) He also holds a PhD in [biophysics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Biophysics "Biophysics") from [Princeton University](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Princeton_University "Princeton University"), where he studied [electrophysiology](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Electrophysiology "Electrophysiology") of [neural circuits](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neural_circuit "Neural circuit").[\[14\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-14) He was a postdoctoral scholar at the [Stanford University School of Medicine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stanford_University_School_of_Medicine "Stanford University School of Medicine").[\[15\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-15)

## Career

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Dario_Amodei&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Career")\]

From November 2014 until October 2015, he worked at [Baidu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Baidu "Baidu").[\[16\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-16) After that, he worked at [Google](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google "Google").[\[17\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-17) In 2016, Amodei joined [OpenAI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/OpenAI "OpenAI").[\[18\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-18)

In 2021, Dario and his sister, Daniela, founded [Anthropic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anthropic "Anthropic") along with other former senior members of OpenAI.[\[19\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-19)[\[20\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-20) The Amodei siblings were among those who left OpenAI due to directional differences.[\[21\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-21)

In November 2023, the board of directors of OpenAI approached Amodei about replacing [Sam Altman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sam_Altman "Sam Altman") and potentially merging the two startups. Amodei declined both offers.[\[22\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-22)

_[Time](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_(magazine) "Time (magazine)")_ magazine listed Amodei as one of [the world's 100 most influential people](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_100 "Time 100") in 2025,[\[23\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-23) and again in 2026 alongside his sister Daniela.[\[24\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-24) He also was named as one of the "Architects of AI" for _Time_'s [Person of the Year](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_Person_of_the_Year "Time Person of the Year").[\[25\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-25)

As of May 2026,[\[update\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Dario_Amodei&action=edit) Anthropic has an estimated value of $965 billion,[\[26\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-26) with _[Forbes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Forbes "Forbes")_ estimating Amodei's net worth to be $7 billion.[\[27\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-27)

## Views

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Dario_Amodei&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Views")\]

### Benefits of AI

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Dario_Amodei&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Benefits of AI")\]

In October 2024, Amodei published an essay entitled "Machines of Loving Grace", in which he speculated about how AI could improve human welfare.[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:1-7) In it, he writes, "I think that most people are underestimating just how radical the upside of AI could be, just as I think most people are underestimating how bad the risks could be."[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:0-6) The essay described a vision of civilization where the risks of AI had been addressed and powerful AI was applied to raise the quality of life for everyone, suggesting that AI could contribute to enormous advances in biology, neuroscience, economic development, global peace, work, and meaning of lives.[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:0-6)

In the article, Amodei also stresses the importance "that democracies have the upper hand on the world stage when powerful AI is created", and argues for an "entente" strategy where a coalition of democracies use AI to achieve a decisive strategic and military advantage over their adversaries, while distributing the benefits to all cooperating democratic nations.[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dario_Amodei#cite_note-:0-6)
