# CBS Media Ventures

#### CBS Media Ventures

CBS Media Ventures is the preeminent company in television syndication. CMV produces or distributes 11 first-run series, including the long-running, critically acclaimed hits "Judge Judy," "Dr. Phil," "Entertainment Tonight," "Wheel of Fortune," "Jeopardy!," "Inside Edition" and "Rachael Ray," as well as the daytime series "The Drew Barrymore Show."

The division also handles national barter advertising sales for Debmar-Mercury’s and Fox’s first-run syndicated programming, and operates Dabl, a multi-platform, advertiser-supported lifestyle network (www.dabl.com). CMV is part of CBS Entertainment Group and a division of Paramount.
