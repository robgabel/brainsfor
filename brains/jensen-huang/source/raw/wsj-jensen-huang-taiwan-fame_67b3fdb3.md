# Nvidia’s Jensen Huang Finds Celebrity Status

## Chip CEO was treated like an A-lister in Taiwan, his place of birth, where locals tracked his every step

By [Liza Lin](https://www.wsj.com/news/author/liza-lin)

Follow
, [Joyu Wang](https://www.wsj.com/news/author/joyu-wang)

TAIPEI—An enthusiastic female fan seeking an autograph on her chest. Thousands braving torrential rains to catch a glimpse of their idol. Admirers clamoring for selfies the moment he appears in public.

As [Nvidia](https://www.wsj.com/market-data/quotes/NVDA)’s market cap [hit $3 trillion](https://www.wsj.com/livecoverage/stock-market-today-dow-sp-500-nasdaq-live-06-05-2024/card/heard-on-the-street-recap-nvidia-joins-the-3-trillion-club-PjlvlRX1Zfag943lbf7M?mod=article_inline), CEO [Jensen Huang](https://www.wsj.com/topics/person/jensen-huang) received a hero’s welcome during a visit to his birth land of [Taiwan](https://www.wsj.com/topics/place/taiwan) to attend an industry event. His [personal star](https://www.wsj.com/business/nvidia-jensen-huang-ceo-ai-chips-89d305de?mod=article_inline)—and his company’s—has never flown higher.
