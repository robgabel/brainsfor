**Gary Vaynerchuk** (born **Gennady Alexandrovich Vaynerchuk**,[\[a\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-2)[\[2\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-3) November 14, 1975; commonly known by his alias **Gary Vee**) is an American businessman, author, speaker, and [internet personality](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet_celebrity "Internet celebrity").[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-businessinsider-4)[\[4\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-NYT2-5)[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-Fortune-6) He is a co-founder of the restaurant reservation software company, [Resy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Resy "Resy"), and Empathy Wines.[\[6\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-cnbcv-7)[\[1\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-foodandwine-1)[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-alleywatch-8) First known as a [wine critic](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wine_critic "Wine critic") who expanded his family's wine business,[\[8\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-cnbc3-9)[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-cnbc2017-10) Vaynerchuk is now more known for his work in [digital marketing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Digital_marketing "Digital marketing") and [social media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_media_marketing "Social media marketing") as the chairman of New York–based communications company VaynerX,[\[10\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-vice.com-11) and as CEO of VaynerX subsidiary VaynerMedia.[\[11\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-recode-12)[\[12\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-13)

## Early life

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gary_Vaynerchuk&action=edit&section=1 "Edit section: Early life")\]

Gennady Alexandrovich Vaynerchuk was born on November 14, 1975, in [Babruysk](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Babruysk "Babruysk") in the [Soviet Union](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_Union "Soviet Union") (today part of [Belarus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belarus "Belarus")),[\[9\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-cnbc2017-10) and [arrived via the U.S. Refugee Resettlement to New York City](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belarusian_Americans_in_New_York_City "Belarusian Americans in New York City") in the [United States](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States "United States") in 1978 at the age of three.[\[5\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-Fortune-6) He is of [Ashkenazi Jewish](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ashkenazi_Jewish "Ashkenazi Jewish") descent.[\[13\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-14) Vaynerchuk was raised in New York City's [Queens](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Queens "Queens") borough and later moved to [Edison, New Jersey](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Edison,_New_Jersey "Edison, New Jersey").[\[14\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-newyorker-15)[\[15\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-abc-16) At age 14, he joined his family's retail-wine business. After his family moved, he graduated from [North Hunterdon High School](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Hunterdon_High_School "North Hunterdon High School").[\[16\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-mycentral-17)

In 1998, Vaynerchuk graduated with a bachelor's degree in management science from [Mount Ida College](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mount_Ida_College "Mount Ida College") in [Newton, Massachusetts](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Newton,_Massachusetts "Newton, Massachusetts").[\[17\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-New_York_Times-18)

## Career

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gary_Vaynerchuk&action=edit&section=2 "Edit section: Career")\]

### Resy

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gary_Vaynerchuk&action=edit&section=3 "Edit section: Resy")\]

Vaynerchuk co-founded [restaurant reservation](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Table_reservation "Table reservation") app [Resy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Resy "Resy"), which was acquired by [American Express](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/American_Express "American Express") in 2019.[\[7\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-alleywatch-8) He is also a co-founder of [winery](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Winery "Winery") Empathy Wines, which was acquired by [Constellation Brands](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Constellation_Brands "Constellation Brands") in 2020.[\[18\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-cnbcnews-19)[\[19\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-thedrinksbusiness-20) Vaynerchuk is the creator and founder behind the VeeFriends1 and VeeFriends2 [non-fungible token](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Non-fungible_token "Non-fungible token") series.[\[20\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-nymag-21)[\[21\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-22)

### Wine Library

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gary_Vaynerchuk&action=edit&section=4 "Edit section: Wine Library")\]

After graduating from college in 1998, Vaynerchuk assumed responsibility of his father's liquor store, Shopper's Discount Liquors. He renamed the store Wine Library, launched sales online, and in 2006 started Wine Library TV, a daily [webcast](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Webcast "Webcast") on YouTube covering wine.[\[22\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-:2-23) He grew the business from $3 million a year to $60 million a year.[\[23\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-businessinsider3-24) In August 2011, Vaynerchuk stepped away from the wine business to build VaynerMedia, a digital ad agency.[\[22\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-:2-23)[\[24\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-25)

### VaynerX

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gary_Vaynerchuk&action=edit&section=5 "Edit section: VaynerX")\]

Vaynerchuk is the [chairman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chairperson "Chairperson") of VaynerX, a communications company that holds media properties and technology companies.[\[25\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-digiday3-26)[\[26\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-entrepreneur5-27)[\[27\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-digiday4-28)

#### VaynerMedia

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gary_Vaynerchuk&action=edit&section=6 "Edit section: VaynerMedia")\]

[![](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3b/Gary_Vaynerchuk_%282015%29.jpg/250px-Gary_Vaynerchuk_%282015%29.jpg)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/File:Gary_Vaynerchuk_(2015).jpg) Gary Vaynerchuk at Internet Week 2015 in New York

In 2009, Vaynerchuk and his brother AJ Vaynerchuk founded VaynerMedia, a [social-media](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Social_media "Social media")-focused digital agency.[\[28\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-:0-29) The company provides social media and strategy services to [Fortune 500](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fortune_500 "Fortune 500") companies.[\[28\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-:0-29)[\[29\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-:1-30) In 2015, VaynerMedia was named one of [Ad Age](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ad_Age "Ad Age")'s A-List agencies.[\[29\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-:1-30) With 600 employees in 2016, VaynerMedia grossed $100 million in revenue.[\[30\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-cnbc-31) The company also partnered with [Vimeo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vimeo "Vimeo") to connect brands and filmmakers for digital content.[\[31\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-adage-32)

#### Gallery Media Group

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gary_Vaynerchuk&action=edit&section=7 "Edit section: Gallery Media Group")\]

In 2017, Vaynerchuk formed The Gallery, later renamed Gallery Media Group, a VaynerX subsidiary company that houses [PureWow](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/PureWow "PureWow"), male-oriented [news outlet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/News_media "News media") ONE37pm.com, and other media properties.[\[32\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-variety3-33)[\[3\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-businessinsider-4)[\[33\]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gary_Vaynerchuk#cite_note-wsj2-34)

### Planet of the Apps

\[ [edit](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Gary_Vaynerchuk&action=edit&section=9 "Edit section: Planet of the Apps")\]
