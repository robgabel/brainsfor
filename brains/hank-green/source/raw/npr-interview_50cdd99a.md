When brothers John and Hank Green realized their relationship had become nothing more than a series of text messages and e-mails, they decided to do something about it: They began posting video blogs for each other on YouTube.

Soon, they had a fan base, and along the way, the two rediscovered their brotherhood.

The Green brothers talk to Andrea Seabrook about what they experienced during their year of video blogging.
