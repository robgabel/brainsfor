## Maybe stories are   data with soul.

The official line: I’m a research professor at the University of Houston, where I hold the Huffington Foundation Endowed Chair. I also hold the position of Professor of Practice in Management at the University of Texas at Austin McCombs School of Business. I’ve spent the past two decades studying courage, vulnerability, shame, and empathy. I’m the author of six #1 _New York Times_ bestsellers, and I’m the host of two award-winning podcasts, [_Unlocking Us_](https://brenebrown.com/podcast-show/unlocking-us/) and [_Dare to Lead_](https://brenebrown.com/podcast-show/dare-to-lead/). I spend most of my time working in organizations around the world, helping develop braver leaders and more courageous cultures. In 2024, I was named Executive Chair of the Center for Daring Leadership at BetterUp.

The bottom line: I believe that you have to walk through vulnerability to get to courage, therefore…embrace the suck. I try to be grateful every day, and my motto right now is “Courage over comfort.”

I do NOT believe that cussing and praying are mutually exclusive. And I absolutely believe that the passing lane is for passing only. I’ve been married to Steve for almost 25 years, and we have two amazing kids, Ellen and Charlie, and a weird Bichon Frisé named Lucy.

## My Story

![Brené born as a baby in the arms of her mom and dad.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/1965.png)

1965

Born in San Antonio, Texas. Thought my name was French until I hitchhiked through Europe after high school and no one in France understood my name. They called me Pamela (from the show _Dallas_). Sigh.

![Brené and Eleanor riding bikes as kids.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/1970.png)

1970

Our first BFF is our first true love. Mine was Eleanor. Living in New Orleans!

![Sibling photo as kids sitting on a gold sofa.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/1974.png)

1974

Oldest of four. Classic over-functioner. Love my brother and sisters!

![Brené high school photo](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/1983.png)

1983

Graduated from high school.

![Brené with short hair and big hoop earrings](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/1984.png)

1984

No comment.

![Brené with big hair in 1986](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/1986.png)

1986

Discover Shaper spray. The higher the hair, the closer to God.

![Brené and Steve when they first met in 1987](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/1987-300x300-1.png)

1987

Met Steve. Worked nights for AT&T taking customer service calls. In Spanish.

![Steve and I date off/on for 7 years and get married!](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/1994-300x300-1.png)

1994

Steve and I date off/on for seven years and get married!

![Brené after her graduation from UT with Steve.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/1995-300x300-1.png)

1995

The 12-year plan pays off! I graduate from UT Austin with my BSW.

![Closeup of Brené and Steve in 1996](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/09/1996-1-300x300-1.png)

1996

We move from San Antonio to Houston. I get my MSW from UH and Steve starts his pediatric residency.

![Ellen as a baby](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/1999.png)

1999

Ellen is born! Heart grows two sizes.

![Brené as a professor at UH during graduation.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2002.png)

2002

I finish my Ph.D. and become a professor at UH. Hooding one of my favorite grad students!

![Ellen making magic dressed as a fairy.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2003.png)

2003

Ellen keeps making magic.

![Cover of Women and Shame](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2004.png)

2004

Finish my first book on shame. Can’t get an agent or a publisher, so I borrow money and self-publish.

![Ellen holding Charlie as a newborn.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2005.png)

2005

Charlie is born! Heart grows two more sizes.

![Steve holding Charlie as a toddler in 2006.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2006.png)

2006

My guys.

![I Thought It Was Just Me book cover](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2007.png)

2007

Penguin buys the self-published book, _Women and Shame,_ and republishes as _I Thought It Was Just Me._

![Young Charlie with a red cowboy hat on.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2008.png)

2008

Mama, don’t let your babies grow up to be cowboys.

![Brené during her Houston Ted Talk](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2010.png)

2010

I give the TedXHouston talk on vulnerability and _The Gifts of Imperfection_ comes out.

![Group photo with Daring Greatly shirts on celebrating the books release.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2012.png)

2012

_Daring Greatly_ comes out. This is my amazing class of graduate social workers at UH!

![Brené on stage - TED Talk](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2012-2.png)

2012

We launch The Daring Way and I give the TED talk on shame.

![Group photo of the BBEARG team wearing matching Rising Strong shirts to celebrate the books Release.](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2015.png)

2015

_Rising Strong_ is released and COURAGEworks is launched.

![Brave Leaders Inc logo](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/2016.png)

2016

I spend most of my time doing leadership development and culture change work. We pilot Brave Leaders Inc.

![](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/2017_Update.png)

2017

Ellen leaves for college, Charlie starts the sixth grade, _Braving the Wilderness_ launches, and we open The Marble Jar.

![](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/2018_Update.png)

2018

_Dare to Lead_ launches along with the hub and ranks #1 on the _New York Times_ bestseller list, as well as _Publishers Weekly_ and the _Wall Street Journal._ Yee. Haw.

![Brené in Times Square with The Call to Courage billboard](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/TimeSquare-Square.jpeg)

2019

Researcher/storyteller doing some trailblazing on Netflix with _The Call to Courage._

![Brené Recording Unlocking Us Podcast](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/1w6a7413_edit_586x586_480.jpeg)

2020

The _Unlocking Us_ and _Dare to Lead_ podcasts launch! Socially distanced but socially connected.

![Tarana Burke and Brené Brown speaking about You Are Your Best Thing](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/BB-TB-Square-V4.jpg)

2021

Tarana Burke and I co-edit and release _You Are Your Best Thing,_ an incredible anthology from a dynamic group of Black writers, organizers, artists, academics, and cultural figures.

![Brené Brown launches Atlas of the Heart](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Atlas_1080.jpg)

2021

_Atlas of the Heart_ launches and my heart is full.

![Brené Brown on stage filming HBO Max series Atlas of The Heart](https://brenebrown.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/02/2022.jpg)

2022

_Atlas of the Heart_ hits HBO Max. All the feels.
