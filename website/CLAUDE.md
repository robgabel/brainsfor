## Design Context

> **Source of truth:** [`../DESIGN.md`](../DESIGN.md) (technical design system) and [`../BRAND.md`](../BRAND.md) (voice, messaging, visual identity). Read both before any design work — they are authoritative; this section is the quick brief. When an Impeccable UX skill runs here (`/critique`, `/audit`, `/polish`, `/bolder`, `/distill`, `/animate`, `/clarify`, `/harden`, `/optimize`, `/extract`, …), it MUST honor the system below instead of defaulting to generic taste. A site that sells "the taste a model can't learn" has to pass its own taste test.

### Users
Builders and operators with high technical fluency — AI power users, ambitious founders/operators, content creators, and developers wiring knowledge into apps. They arrived to *evaluate*, not to be sold to. They want to see the machinery (atoms, connections, JSON, the install command, confidence scores), judge it for themselves, and decide. Respect their intelligence; never coddle, never hype.

### Brand Personality
"The rare bookshop of AI." Confident, not loud. Precise, not clinical. Intellectual, not academic. Warm, not soft. Provocative, not edgy. Builder-first, not consumer-first. The product sells *taste a model can't learn* — so the interface itself is the first proof of that taste. We are NOT: corporate, hype-driven, cute/playful, apologetic, or "generic AI."

### Aesthetic Direction
Stripe-inspired premium knowledge aesthetic. Light-first canvas (`#ffffff`) with warm-paper (`#f8f7f4`) alternating sections for editorial rhythm. Space Grotesk weight-300 at display sizes with negative letter-spacing — "whispered authority," lightness as intellectual confidence. Inter for body at 1.60 line-height. JetBrains Mono for all code/commands. Brain Indigo (`#6366f1`) is the singular accent. Indigo-tinted multi-layer shadows (`rgba(99,102,241,…)`). Knowledge Gold (`#d97706`) for premium moments ONLY (scores, featured badges) — never CTAs or links.
**Anti-references (never produce these):** generic SaaS dashboards, AI-slop visuals (glowing brains, neural nets, robot hands), corporate stock photography, gradients-for-gradients'-sake, decorative blobs, pill-shaped buttons.

### Design Principles
1. **Lightness is confidence.** Weight-300 display, generous whitespace (80px+ between sections), restraint over decoration. The risk on this site is over-decorating, not under — when in doubt, reach for `/distill` and `/quieter` before `/bolder` and `/colorize`. Indigo restraint *is* the taste signal.
2. **Show the machinery.** Atoms, connections, JSON schema, install command, confidence tiers, source links — visible, never hidden. Transparency IS the brand.
3. **The product is the visual.** Terminal demos, the live playground, and `explore.html` ARE the hero imagery. Never substitute decorative art for a running demo. Do beats Show beats Explain.
4. **One accent that acts.** Brain Indigo is the only interactive/CTA color. Gold is decorative only. No warm/soft CTA colors, ever.
5. **Specifics over superlatives; the first mile over everything.** Exact numbers ("1,086 atoms, 1,174 connections"), never "powerful"/"comprehensive." The first 30 seconds is the proof — invest disproportionately there.

### Hard Constraints (non-negotiable — DESIGN.md §7, BRAND.md §11)
- Headings: Deep Ink (`#0f172a`), never pure black. Body: `#64748b`. Labels: `#334155`.
- Shadows always indigo-tinted, never neutral gray.
- Buttons: 8px radius. Pills (`9999px`) for badges/tags ONLY — never buttons.
- Space Grotesk for display only; Inter for body. Never body copy in Space Grotesk.
- Card/button border-radius stays 6–12px.
- Banned UI/marketing words: revolutionary, game-changing, unlock, leverage, seamless, solution, empower, AI clone, chatbot, thought leader, insights (mass noun). Full list: BRAND.md §4.
